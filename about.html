About Me

In April 2020, I started looking into a career change. I had spent my time since graduating from the University of Richmond playing professional football in the NFL, XFL, and AAF. I had decided it was time to hang up the cleats and take a leap of faith into software development. Since then, I've learned and grown both technically and personally. I've met some inspiring individuals, created lots of 'firsts', and overcame a lot of self doubt and failure that I was not used to. I became passionate for an industry that I have barely scratched the surface of and I continue to be amazed by the power of software. This site will serve as a portfolio to give you a look at what I have been working on.

I'm passionate about people, innovation, and accomplishing personal goals. My background in football has taught me that anything is possible with hardwork, persistance, and grit. When I am not coding, I love to play golf, run, and watch and rate movies (I am currently working on designing an app to search movies I have rated and see what score I gave them).
